About Blockchain Voting

The technology behind blockchain and Aadhaar verification are used to simplify the process of the voting system. The Aadhaar number has to be fetched and verified with the database after entering every vote on the machine. The information has to be verified for the vote of an individual to be accepted by EVM and stored in block chain ledger.
